Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

SouvenirBird is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your family memories are deeply personal and should remain yours alone.

Data Storage

All your memories, photos, and data are stored exclusively on your device. SouvenirBird does not use cloud storage, does not sync data to any server, and does not transmit your memories anywhere.

No Account Required

SouvenirBird does not require you to create an account. We do not collect email addresses, usernames, passwords, or any personal identification information.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party tracking tools. Your usage of the app is completely private.

Permissions

SouvenirBird may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your memories and photos on your device
  • Camera: To capture photos for your memories (optional)

These permissions are used only for the core functionality of the app. No data is ever transmitted outside your device.

Third-Party Services

SouvenirBird does not integrate with any third-party services, social media platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

While SouvenirBird is designed for parents to document their children's lives, it does not collect any data. All memories remain on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since the app works offline, please check this website periodically for updates.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the information provided on our Imprint page.

Designed for parents, from parents.
